Sinangag

Cooking time: 15 minutes

Recipe

  • Break cold rice to seperate grains in a bowl.
  • Heat about ¼ cup of oil in a wide skillet over low heat. Add garlic and cook. Stir occasionally until it reaches a golden brown color. Remove the garlic with a slotted spoon and drain it on a paper towel.
  • Remove the excess oil, but leave about 1 to 2 tablespoons. Raise the heat higher while swirlign the oil to coat the pan's surface.
  • After adding rice, cook it and spread it on the surface of the pan for 45 seconds until the grains start to sizzle. Toss it to redistribute, repeat until it is heated enough.
  • Return ¾ of the garlic. Toss until the fried rice is evenly distributed.
  • Season with salt and pepper to your liking.
  • When serving, transfer to a platter and scatter garlic bits.
Ingredients
  • 4 cups cold, cooked white rice
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • 1 head garlic, peeled and minced
  • salt and pepper to taste
